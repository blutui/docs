## Create a new Role 

This is where you create a new Role.

![Role section](https://cdn.blutui.com/uploads/assets/Help/agency/organisation-settings/creating-a-role/screenshot-2026-01-15-at-92917-am.png?format=webp&width=800)

Click on the `Create role` button on the top right-hand section of the screen.

![Create new role](https://cdn.blutui.com/uploads/assets/Help/agency/organisation-settings/creating-a-role/screenshot-2026-01-15-at-92936-am.png?format=webp&width=800)

You can now select the appropriate permissions for `Read`, `Write`, `Delete` for the different features within the agency console.
