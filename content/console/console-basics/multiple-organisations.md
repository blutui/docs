## Move Between Organisations

### Easily jump from agency to agency with Blutui

The organisations that you belong to are all displayed in the drop down box in the right hand corner; you can easily jump from agency to agency. If you're working with multiple different agencies or different organisations within an agency, organisations make it easy to switch from one to the other.
